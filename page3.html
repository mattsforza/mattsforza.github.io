Music Preferences

My favorite genre of music is rap & hip hop. One of my favorite artists is Drake. My favorite album by Drake is his most recent one "Scorpion" which released June 29, 2018. The album has received nearly 1 billion streams worldwide.
Along with rapping, Drake produces music, hosts award shows, and does some acting. He was known as Jimmy Brooks on the show "Degrassi" before ever creating music. I prefer Drake as a musician and his talents have increased almost vertically since the start to his career.
My favorite song currently by Drake is God's Plan. The track released in February of 2018 and has reached 800 million views within the same year.
